# Heart Failure — Classification

Heart failure with reduced ejection fraction (HFrEF) is defined as a left
ventricular ejection fraction (LVEF) below 40 percent. Heart failure with
preserved ejection fraction (HFpEF) is defined as an LVEF of 50 percent or
higher. The range of 41 to 49 percent is termed mildly reduced.

Guideline-directed medical therapy for HFrEF includes an ARNI, a beta-blocker,
a mineralocorticoid receptor antagonist, and an SGLT2 inhibitor.
