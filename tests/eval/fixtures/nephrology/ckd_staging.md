# Chronic Kidney Disease — Staging

Chronic kidney disease (CKD) is staged from G1 to G5 according to the estimated
glomerular filtration rate (eGFR). Stage G1 is an eGFR of 90 or above with
evidence of kidney damage; stage G5 is an eGFR below 15, which is kidney
failure. Albuminuria is graded separately from A1 to A3.
