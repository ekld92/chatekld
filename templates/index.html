How to Use ChatEKLD

Pick a provider. Choose Ollama or LM Studio in the sidebar, then select a chat model and an embedding model. OCR and Vision have their own provider and model selectors below.

Obsidian Agent. Set your vault folder with “Change…”, then click “Index Vault”. The first index can be long for large vaults; you can Pause (resumable, preserves work) or Cancel (still resumable) at any time. After indexing, ask questions in the input bar — answers are grounded in your notes and PDFs.

Single Paper. Click the upload area to pick a PDF. Choose a summary style, document type, audience, language, and (optionally) a focus question and LLM parameters. Click “Summarise” and then “Export TXT” or “Export MD” to save the result to your Downloads.

Tips. If you change the embedding model after indexing, re-index from scratch — mixing embeddings yields a useless index. PDFs over 5000 pages are skipped; PDFs between 1000 and 5000 pages are extracted in 1000-page chunks. Scanned PDFs use the OCR model as a fallback when no text layer is found.

Reset. “Reset App Data” clears uploads and the Obsidian index. You can also delete the saved config and feedback file from the reset flow if needed.
